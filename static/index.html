Financial Literacy

Research has shown there are 30 million households unbanked or underbanked. To bring more awareness, we must understand why they are underbanked. One of the biggest barriers is consumer misunderstanding and misinformation about bank services.

Bring Awareness with EZ BANKING

EZ BANKING will help provide resources for the underbanked. It provides online-banking targeted to those who don't have Savings or Checkings account.

Learn More

Easy Access to Banking

The great majority of underbanked households use cash and nonbank money orders to pay bills. EZ BANKING will encourage customers into a range of payment options that extend electronic payments.
